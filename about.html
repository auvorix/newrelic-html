About Clearwater Consulting

Independent advisory firm founded in Dublin in 2008.

Our Story

Clearwater Consulting was founded in 2008 by Margaret Hennessy and David Lohan, two former partners at international consulting firms who believed that mid-market businesses deserved the same quality of strategic advice as the largest corporations — without the overhead and bureaucracy.

From a two-person office on Baggot Street, we have grown to a team of 34 consultants serving clients across Ireland, the UK, and continental Europe. We have maintained our independence throughout, taking no equity positions and accepting no retainer arrangements that could compromise objectivity.

Today, Clearwater is recognised by the Irish Management Institute as one of Ireland's leading independent advisory firms, with particular strength in financial services, professional services, and technology-enabled businesses.

Our Values

Independence

We are privately owned, debt-free, and accept no commissions or referral fees. Our only obligation is to our clients.

Rigour

Every recommendation is grounded in data, tested against alternatives, and stress-tested with our peer review process before it reaches a client.

Accountability

We measure our success by client outcomes, not by engagement duration. We set targets and track them.

Candour

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Honest advice, clearly delivered.
